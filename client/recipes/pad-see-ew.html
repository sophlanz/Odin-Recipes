Pad See Ew

Description

Pad See Ew is a Thai dish very similar to Pad Thai, but they differ in the type of noodle. Personally I preder Pad See Ew to Pad Thai, as I prefer the wide rice noodle gooeyness. It's the perfect dish to fill you up when you're feeling especially hungry, but be careful if you need to return to work afterwards, as this plate might give you a little bit of the itis.

Ingredients

  • chicken or tofu
  • sesame oil
  • peanut oil
  • garlic
  • red chillies
  • green onions
  • rice noodles
  • Chinese brocolli
  • dark soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • fish sauce
  • brown sugar
  • vinegar
  • egg
